How It Works

1
Encrypted in your browser. Your secret is encrypted with AES-256-GCM before it ever leaves your device. The encryption key is never sent to our server.
2
Key stays in the link. The encryption key lives in the URL fragment (#), which browsers never send to servers. Only the link holder can decrypt.
3
Destroyed after viewing. Once the recipient opens the link and reveals the secret, the encrypted data is permanently deleted. No one — including us — can recover it.
We can't read your secrets. Ever.
